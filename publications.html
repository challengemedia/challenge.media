Chicago Journal

The Chicago Journal is a general interest, digital publication focused on the political, cultural, and economic issues of the city of Chicago and the surrounding metro area.

Chicago is home to Challenge Media and the Chicago Journal is our flagship site. It's where we're providing proof of concept for our publication model and the foundation upon which we hope to grow.
