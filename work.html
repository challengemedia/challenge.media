Chicago Journal

The Chicago Journal is a general interest, digital publication with a focus on the political and cultural issues relevant to the city of Chicago and the surrounding metro area.

Chicago is home to Challenge Media and the Chicago Journal is our flagship site. It's where we hope to provide proof of concept for our publication model to grow.
